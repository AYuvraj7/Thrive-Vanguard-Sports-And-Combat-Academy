Personal Story
Black Belt Karate State Karate Player National Wushu Sports Administrator Fitness Leader Academy Founder

Yuvraj Singh Thakur is a decorated martial artist, sports administrator and community leader with over two decades of dedicated experience in martial arts and sports development in Madhya Pradesh.

His journey into martial arts began in 2005 with participation in the EXPO International Sports Meet, which ignited a lifelong passion for combat sports and athletic excellence. By 2006, he was competing at the Junior National Wushu level, representing Madhya Pradesh on the national stage.

After earning his prestigious Black Belt in Karate in 2008 and competing at the MP State Karate Championship, Yuvraj channeled his expertise into training and administration. In 2017, he served as Team Manager for the MP State Wushu Championship, demonstrating his leadership in sports management.

In 2025, he founded the Thrive Skills Educational Society and established Thrive Vanguard Sports & Combat Academy in Chachai, District Anuppur — creating a world-class training facility. His Fit Chachai Mission in 2026 further reflects his commitment to community wellness and sports promotion.
