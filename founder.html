A Journey of Dedication & Excellence

Yuvraj Singh Thakur is a dedicated martial artist, sports administrator and community leader who has devoted over two decades to the development of sports and martial arts culture in Madhya Pradesh. His journey began in 2005 with participation in the EXPO International Sports Meet, which sparked a lifelong passion for martial arts excellence.

Earning his Black Belt in Karate in 2008 after years of rigorous training, Yuvraj went on to represent Madhya Pradesh at state and national levels. His expertise in Karate and Wushu, combined with his engineering education, has given him a unique analytical approach to sports training and athlete development.

In 2025, he channeled his experience and vision into founding the Thrive Vanguard Sports & Combat Academy under the Thrive Skills Educational Society, creating a world-class training facility in Chachai, District Anuppur. His Fit Chachai Mission in 2026 further demonstrates his commitment to community wellness and sports promotion.
