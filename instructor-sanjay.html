Introduction
Black Belt Karate Multi-Sport Athlete National Level State Champion Krida Bharti Community Leader

Sanjay Jogi is a highly accomplished martial artist, multi-sport athlete and dedicated instructor at Thrive Vanguard Sports & Combat Academy. With a rare combination of achievements across six different sports disciplines, he brings exceptional versatility, technical depth and competitive experience to his role as a Karate instructor.

A Black Belt in Karate with three State level tournament achievements, Sanjay has also competed at State level in Wushu (3 times), Boxing (1 State), and Power Lifting (3 State) — demonstrating extraordinary athletic commitment across multiple combat and strength sports.

His most distinguished achievement is his National level participation in Taekwondo as Team Coach and Manager — a testament to his leadership capabilities and deep understanding of combat sports administration.

Beyond sports, Sanjay serves as Treasurer of Krida Bharti, actively contributing to sports promotion and community development. At Thrive Vanguard Academy, he brings this wealth of experience to develop the next generation of martial artists and athletes.
